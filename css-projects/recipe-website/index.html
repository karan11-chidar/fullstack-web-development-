Kaju Curry

Ingredients:-

  • Kaju
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices
  • Fresh Cream

Procedure:-

  1. Step 1:- First fry the kaju in little oil and keep it aside.

  2. Step 2:- Second, in a pan, add oil and sauté onions until golden brown.

  3. Step 3:- Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
